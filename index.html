Understanding Credit Card Generators

Namsogen's Credit Card Generator is a powerful tool designed for testing and development purposes. Our generator creates valid credit card numbers that pass basic validation algorithms but cannot be used for actual transactions.

How Our Credit Card Generator Works

Namsogen utilizes the Luhn algorithm (also known as mod 10) to generate valid credit card numbers. This algorithm is used by all major credit card companies to validate card numbers.

When you enter a BIN (Bank Identification Number), our system creates card numbers that match that specific pattern. You can use "x" as a wildcard for random digits in your BIN input.

Features of Our Tool

  • Custom BIN Support: Generate cards based on specific Bank Identification Numbers
  • Expiration Date Options: Set custom or random expiration dates
  • CCV Generation: Automatically create valid security codes based on card type
  • Bulk Generation: Create multiple card numbers at once
  • Copy Functionality: Easily copy results to clipboard

Common Uses

Our credit card generator is commonly used for:

  • Testing e-commerce websites
  • Validating payment forms
  • Software development
  • Data entry training

Important Disclaimer

The credit card numbers generated by this tool are mathematically valid but not connected to any real accounts or financial institutions. Using these numbers for fraudulent purposes is illegal and unethical. This tool is provided for legitimate testing and development purposes only.
