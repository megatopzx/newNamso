Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how File Encryptor, Namso Gen, and Bin Save handle your information.

1. Information Collection

Our suite of tools - File Encryptor, Namso Gen, and Bin Save - processes all data locally in your browser. We prioritize your privacy by ensuring that no personal data is collected, stored, or transmitted to our servers.

2. Local Storage

We may use browser local storage for temporary, non-sensitive application settings across our tools. This storage is strictly limited to enhancing user experience and does not store personal or sensitive information.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, adhering to their respective privacy policies:

  • CryptoJS: For secure encryption/decryption functions
  • Font Awesome: For interface icons
  • CloudFlare CDN: For delivering static resources

4. Specific Tool Privacy

File Encryptor

Encrypts and decrypts files entirely in the browser with zero server-side data transmission.

Namso Gen

Generates test credit card numbers for development and testing purposes. No real financial data is stored or transmitted.

Bin Save

Allows local storage of BIN (Bank Identification Number) information in the browser's local storage. Data is private and not shared externally.

5. Security Commitment

We implement state-of-the-art encryption technologies to protect your data across all our tools. However, we recommend users maintain their own data backups and follow best security practices.

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are designed for users 13 years and older. We do not intentionally collect or maintain data from individuals under 13.

7. International Users

Our services comply with international data protection regulations, including GDPR and CCPA principles.

8. Policy Updates

We continuously update our privacy practices. Users are encouraged to review this policy periodically for any changes.

9. Contact Information
